What is TI7? What is DotA?

TI7 is the abbreviation for The International 7, which is a yearly tournament to find the best DotA team in the world. It attracts millions of viewers, and is considered one the games at the forefront of eSports. TI7 is sponsored by Valve, the creator of Steam an online game distrubution platform and several iconic games such as Half-life, Portal, Counter-Strike, and Team Fortress 2. TI7 is focused on DotA 2, one of their newer games and the biggest one. Also the best one
